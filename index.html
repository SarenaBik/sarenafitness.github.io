Meet Your Trainer

Hello, I'm Sarena.

I believe every woman deserves a fitness experience built entirely around her — her schedule, her goals, her home. No crowded gyms, no intimidation, no wasted commute.

As a certified personal trainer specializing in women's fitness, I design every session around you. Whether you're a complete beginner or returning after time off, I'll meet you exactly where you are and guide you to where you want to be.

I serve women across Vaughan, Thornhill, and Richmond Hill — and I come straight to your door.
